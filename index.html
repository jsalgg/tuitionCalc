*DISCLAIMER:

This is not a bill. This is only an estimate. Total tuition and fees are estimates and are subject to change. Other costs such as lab fees, proctored exam fees, books, supplies and other materials are not included in this calculation.

Distance Learning Fee: Florida Statute 1009.24(17) (b) allows the university to charge a Distance Learning Fee for all online courses that generate fundable student credit hours. The fee is charged per credit, and is subject to change depending on the amount of credits taken.

FIU has multi session terms during the Fall, Spring and Summer semesters. Enrollment and tuition are determined based on the total enrollment for all sessions in a term. Multi session terms do not affect the prices reflected above as these do not affect the cost per credit.

One Card: This is an annual fee, not related to your per credit fee, therefore, it is included in the total tuition but not listed above.

† These are fees that FIU Online students have waived by being part of a fully online program
